ABOUT US

Perpetual Ndegwa & Company Advocates is a dynamic law firm headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. With a commitment to providing forward-thinking legal solutions, we address a wide array of legal challenges both locally and internationally. Our firm specializes in key practice areas including commercial and corporate law, conveyancing, family law, criminal law, and employment law. Our mission is to deliver compassionate and pragmatic legal resolutions, while our vision propels us to be the leading choice for legal services through innovative approaches to practice.

Our Practices

  • Conveyancing

    Our experienced team facilitates seamless property transactions by providing expert guidance in property transfers, acquisitions, leases, and mortgages. We ensure clients' interests are safeguarded throughout the entire process.

  • Family Law

    We offer comprehensive family law services, including divorce, child custody, adoption, and property settlements. Our empathetic approach and legal expertise provide clients with sound solutions during emotionally challenging times.

  • Criminal Law

    With a staunch commitment to justice, we provide legal representation for individuals facing criminal charges. Our knowledgeable attorneys strive to uphold the rights of our clients while ensuring a fair and just legal process.

  • Law of Succession

    Our firm assists clients in matters related to inheritance, wills, trusts, and estate planning. We work to preserve legacies and fulfil the wishes of individuals in accordance with the law.

  • Compliance & Audits

    We support businesses and individuals in navigating complex legal landscapes. Our team conducts comprehensive legal audits and ensures regulatory compliance, minimizing legal risks and fostering ethical practices.

  • Employment Law

    We offer expert advice on labour disputes, employment contracts, and workplace regulations. Our aim is to create harmonious employer-employee relationships while safeguarding the rights of both parties.

  • Corporate Law

    Our legal experts provide strategic guidance to businesses, assisting in formation, contracts, mergers, acquisitions, and general corporate governance. We contribute to the growth and stability of enterprises across sectors.

  • Legal Aid

    Committed to social responsibility, we provide legal aid services to those who may not have easy access to legal representation. Our aim is to ensure justice is accessible to all, irrespective of their socio-economic background.
